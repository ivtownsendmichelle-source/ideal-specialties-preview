Who we are

A woman-owned, family-run food broker

We have one job: getting good food to the people who need it. Whether you need a pallet or a truckload, we are here to help.

I Deal Specialties is led by Lorie Gelb, President. Lorie brings more than 30 years in the wholesale food business, including years as a senior manager and owner of a family-owned, independent food purveyor in Ohio. That family-business background is the heart of how we work: personal, dependable, and in it for the long haul.

We are big enough to meet the need and small enough to care about the mission. Food banks work with us because we pick up the phone, we do what we say, and we treat your budget like it is our own.
